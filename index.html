    Modeling ‘Leave’ Vote Proportions in the 2016 UK Referendum Report

    This report investigates what shaped ward-level ‘Leave’ vote shares in the UK’s 2016 EU referendum and estimates results for wards without official counts. Drawing on demographic, socioeconomic, housing, and regional data, we compare linear, generalized linear, and generalized additive models, selecting the approach that best captures non-linear patterns. We outline key drivers—age structure, ethnicity, tenure, education, employment, and deprivation
